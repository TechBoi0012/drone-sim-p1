Hotkeys

  • [C]: Toggle Camera View (FPV / Chase Camera)
  • [M]: Toggle Flight Mode (ANGLE = Stabilized / ACRO = Pro Rate)
  • [R]: Reset Drone position (restarts timer)
  • [ESC]: Pause / Toggle Menu

Acro vs Angle Mode

Angle Mode is auto-leveling. The drone will automatically tilt to the angle dictated by the pitch/roll keys, and return to flat hover when released. Perfect to get your bearings.

Acro Mode is manual rate control. Pressing a pitch or roll key causes the drone to spin constantly in that direction. When you release the key, the drone stays at that angle! To level out, you must apply the counter tilt. This matches real acrobatic FPV racing.
